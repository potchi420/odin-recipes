Recipes for Filipino Pork Barbeque

Description

Filipino Pork Barbeque is a popular street food made from marinated pork skewers grilled to perfection. The marinade typically includes a combination of soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, brown sugar, and spices, giving the pork a sweet and savory flavor. It is often served with rice and a side of dipping sauce.

  1. 1 lb pork shoulder, cut into bite-sized pieces
  2. 1/4 cup soy sauce
  3. 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  7. 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  8. Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes
